# Palmer Penguins dataset

The dataset was collected and made available by Dr. Kristen Gorman and the Palmer Station, Antarctica. 

The dataset contains 344 observations of 8 variables:

* *species*: penguin species (Ad√©lie, Chinstrap or Gentoo)
* *island*: island in Palmer Archipelago, Antarctica (Biscoe, Dream or Torgersen)
* *bill_length_mm*: bill length (in millimeters)
* *bill_depth_mm*: bill depth (in millimeters)
* *flipper_length_mm*: flipper length (in millimeters, integer)
* *body_mass_g*: body mass (in grams, integer)
* *sex*: penguin sex (female or male)
* *year*: study year (2007, 2008, or 2009)

Further information on the dataset are available at https://allisonhorst.github.io/palmerpenguins/index.html.
